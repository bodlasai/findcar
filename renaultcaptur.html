RENAULT CAPTUR

                                  RENAULT CAPTUR

Rs 10.0 - 14.1 Lakh*

                                                                      
The Renault Captur has been launched at a starting price of Rs 9.99 lakh, going all the way up to Rs 13.88 lakh (ex-showroom New Delhi). It is available in a total of 10 variants – four for petrol and six for diesel – including the dual-tone options, which cost Rs 17,000 extra over the regular model. The India-spec model gets an exclusive 'Platine' edition, which is only available with the diesel engine. Sharing the platform with the Duster, the Renault Captur borrows its 1.5-litre petrol and diesel engines as well, with a manual transmission option. The Renault Captur delivers a claimed mileage of up to 20.37kmpl for the diesel variants and 13.87kmpl for the petrol. To know how it drives, do check out our detailed Renault Captur Review Video. The Renault Captur SUV is positioned over the Duster and comes across as a relatively premium product. Standard features on the Captur will include dual airbags, ABS, alloy wheels, and an entertainment system. Others include quilted leather upholstery, cruise control, hill start assist, full-LED headlamps, LED fog lamps with cornering function, 7-inch touchscreen system with navigation and rear camera support, push-button start, and more. Renault offers two preset customisation options for the Captur along with more. Click here to get the details. Being an SUV, the Renault Captur features a class-leading ground clearance of 210mm. It has a boot space of 392 litres. The Renault Captur goes against the Hyundai Creta and the Maruti S-Cross facelift in the compact SUV segment.
