Maruti Suzuki has launched the S-Cross facelift at Rs 8.49 lakh (ex-showroom, New Delhi). Check out more details here. With this facelift, Maruti has ditched the 1.6-litre diesel engine and only offers the relatively underpowered 1.3-litre diesel now. Further, the 1.3-litre diesel now comes with Suzuki's mild-hybrid SHVS tech. The S-Cross facelift delivers a claimed mileage of 25.1kmpl - an increase of 1.45kmpl. The S-Cross facelift is available in four variants – Sigma, Delta, Zeta and Alpha – same as before.
