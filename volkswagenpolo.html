Under its 'Volksfest' festive bonanza, VW has launched an Anniversary Edition of the Polo based on its Comfortline trim. It comes with cosmetic changes inside and out. Check out more info here. Volkswagen has already started production of its next-gen Polo hatchback and a production version of the same was recently spied in Brazil. The car is likely to make India debut by second half of 2018. The present fifth-gen Polo is offered in different variants and differently tuned engines. The Volkswagen Polo 1.5-litre diesel delivers an excellent mileage of 20.14kmpl while the same engine in GT series offers fuel efficiency of 21.49kmpl. Contrarily, the petrol Polo returns with figures of 16.47kmpl with its 1.2-litre engine. The Polo GT TSI offers a mileage of 17.21kmpl. It rivals with the Hyundai Elite i20, Maruti Swift, Ford Figo and Maruti Baleno.
