Welcome to findcar.com - India's largest auto media vehicle. findcar has always striven to serve car buyers and owners in the most comprehensive and convenient way possible. We provide a platform where car buyers and owners can research buy and come together to discuss and talk about their cars.

our mission:

Our mission is to bring joy and delight into car buying and ownership. To achieve this goal, we aim to empower Indian consumers to make informed car buying and ownership decisions with exhaustive and un-biased information on cars through our expert reviews, owner reviews, detailed specifications and comparisons. We understand that a car is by and large the second-most expensive asset a consumer associates his lifestyle with.

A Brief History:

findcar was launched in 2017 by BODLA SAi. Since then we have been credited with several initiatives for Indian car consumers. In 2017, we became the first website in India to consolidate used car inventory across dealers and present it to car buyers as an online marketplace.
